IT ALL BEGAN IN A GARAGE...

Back in 2024, HyperMenu was just a few lines of Python script designed to make our gaming setups easier. We were tired of "bloatware" that slowed down our systems.

We spent 500+ sleepless nights rewriting the core engine to ensure that HyperMenu would never use more than 1% of your CPU. Today, it’s a global standard for UI efficiency.
