How it Started

HyperMenu didn’t start as a professional tool; it was born out of frustration. Our founders needed a faster way to navigate complex game menus and access tools. We started with simple scripts to automate keystrokes, but they were limited, clunky, and slow.

In 2024, the idea for HyperMenu was solidified. The goal was to build a system that was truly configurable, built for speed (low-latency), and could be used by anyone, from casual users to developers.

Today, HyperMenu has evolved into a full documentation framework, providing seamless integration across multiple platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is HyperMenu free?

Yes, HyperMenu is free for personal use. We also offer professional features for developers and power users.

How is HyperMenu faster?

Our menu system is built from the ground up for low latency, optimized with efficient code that ensures almost instantaneous response times.

What platforms are supported?

HyperMenu supports many platforms including Windows, macOS, and Linux. We also plan to release extensions for various software.
